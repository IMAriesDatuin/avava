Our Technology
{{> block-cap-bottom}}

Three-Dimensional Spatial Selectivity

Our spatially selective technology utilizes X, Y and Z axes to focus on hyperpigmenetd areas and damaged skin cells with more precision than ever before. AVAVA targets hyperpigmentation below the epidermis - along the vertical Z axis - eliminating the need for surface-level contrast and making the laser highly effective even for patients with darker skin.

Highest Fluence at the Focal Plane

Innovative focusing optics with a cooling window keep fluence and intensity at the surface low. An approach that avoids heating and damaging the epidermis. The highest fluence occurs at the focal plane providing more power where it's needed and targeting hyperpigmentation at its roots.

Optimized treatments

AVAVA's 3D precision reduces damage to surrounding tissues maximizing patient safety and recovery time.

Setting a new bar in patient safety

Low heat at the surface keeps patients comfortable during treatments and ensures minimal to no damage to visible skin surfaces.

Unparalled customization

AVAVA's interface can also allow for adjustments in the laser's position and focus along each axis, lending a high level of customization to each patient's treatment pattern.

High-level focus & precision

With AVAVA's laser energy focused on tissues below the surface, we can heat hyperpgimentation more effectively.

Pilot clinical studies show unprecedented pigment reduction within one week of treatment

Early results from tests and clinical trails show a clear dose-response relationship allowing optimization of treatment lengths and schedules for patients of multiple skin types with varies concerns. We are developing several configurations for our technology which can suit different grades of hyperpigmentation severity, hyperpigmentation on sensitive areas, patients with simultaneous skin concerns and more. We see great potential for use in other dermatological indications and in other medical and technological fields.
