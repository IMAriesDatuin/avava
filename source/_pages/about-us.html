Who we are

AVAVA is the latest brainchild of Blossom Innovations, a biomedical technology incubator based in Waltham, Massachusetts. Blossom was founded in 2011 by a handful of bright minds out of Boston’s top medical institutions — a group of friends and colleagues whose creative mindset and professional experience led them naturally toward the development of innovative solutions for unmet clinical needs. With a proven history of harnessing the latest medical breakthroughs into real-world clinical treatments, Blossom Innovations has been a key player in such leading technologies as cryolipolysis (CoolSculpting), laser skin resurfacing and laser hair removal.

The diverse team dedicated to AVAVA includes medical professionals in dermatology and photomedicine, PhD-level researchers in biomedicine and physics, business and finance experts and top-level support staff. They have built AVAVA from the ground up, from recognizing the need for new laser technologies to conceptualizing our novel approach to creating and testing the AVAVA system. Every person behind AVAVA adds passion and determination to our mission and goals.

Why we do it

We believe that people and patients come first, so our vision began with real human problems: hyperpigmentation and melasma. These skin conditions consistently rank in the top five skin disorders worldwide but have few damage-free and globally effective solutions. We created AVAVA with the single, uncompromising goal of achieving meaningful clinical therapies for all skin types, even darker skin, that are safe and tolerable.

Our technology marks the beginning of a new paradigm for laser skin therapy — one that utilizes three-dimensional, spatially selective capabilities for highly selective tissue treatment, and one that champions safety and efficacy for patients of all skin types. With plans for expansion to worldwide markets and new clinical applications, we have only just begun to explore our full potential.
