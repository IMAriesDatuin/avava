AVAVA is mission-driven skin therapy

We believe that people and patients come first, so our vision began with real human problems: hyperpigmentation and melasma. These skin conditions consistently rank in the top five skin disorders worldwide but have few damage-free and globally effective solutions. We created AVAVA with the single, uncompromising goal of achieving meaningful clinical therapies for all skin types, even darker skin, that are safe and tolerable.

Our technology marks the beginning of a new paradigm for laser skin therapy — one that utilizes three-dimensional, spatially selective capabilities for highly selective tissue treatment, and one that champions safety and efficacy for patients of all skin types. With plans for expansion to worldwide markets and new clinical applications, we have only just begun to explore our full potential.

Bringing all-new skin treatments to an universal market

Dark-skinned individuals comprise most of the world’s population and struggle with high rates of pigmentary conditions, yet current therapies for hyperpigmentation and melasma are ineffective on dark skin. AVAVA fills this void in the market for skin therapy, providing diverse patients with long-sought relief from acne- and injury-induced post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation and melasma.

Three-dimensional spatial selection

With the unparalleled precision of three-dimensional spatial selection, our technology focuses on pigmentation below the epidermis rather than relying on surface contrast for targeting.

Prevents damage

This novel approach prevents heat damage to the epidermis and surrounding tissues with low fluence at the skin surface, while creating an effective solution for dark-skinned patients with low skin-to-hyperpigmentation contrast.
